My Story

From Curious Coder to
AI SEO Engineer.

My journey into search started in 2016, when I built my first WordPress site to teach myself web development. Within three months it was ranking — almost by accident — and that single observation changed my career path. I realized SEO wasn't marketing fluff. It was an engineering problem.

Over the next 8+ years I worked across e-commerce, SaaS, real estate, fintech, and media — first as an SEO analyst, then as a team lead, and now as a senior AI SEO consultant for US-based brands. Along the way I taught myself Python, picked up data science, and started writing automation scripts that did the work of three junior analysts in an afternoon.

Today I bring that same engineering mindset to every client engagement — whether that's a Manhattan SaaS company chasing enterprise keywords, a Texas e-commerce brand rebuilding its tech stack, or a California fintech recovering from a Google core update. My job is to translate algorithm changes, AI search behavior, and user intent into measurable revenue growth for American businesses.
